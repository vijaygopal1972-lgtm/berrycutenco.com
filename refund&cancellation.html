Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: November 16, 2025

Cancellation Policy

Orders can only be canceled within *12 hours* of placement. Since all our Mystery Scoops are prepared quickly to ensure timely dispatch, we cannot guarantee cancellation requests submitted after this window. To request a cancellation, please contact us immediately via WhatsApp or email with your Order ID.

Refund Policy

Due to the nature of our *Mystery Scoop* product, which involves randomized items, *all sales are considered final and non-refundable.* We do not offer refunds or exchanges simply if you do not like the items received.

Exceptions (Damaged or Incorrect Items)

We take extreme care in packaging, but if you receive an item that is damaged or incorrect (i.e., not a Mystery Scoop item), please follow these steps:

  • You must notify us within *48 hours* of delivery.
  • Provide clear, unboxing video evidence of the damaged or incorrect item.
  • We will assess the damage and, if approved, offer a replacement for the specific damaged item or a store credit equivalent to its value. We do not offer full refunds for the entire scoop in these cases.

Refund Processing

Approved store credits or refunds (in the rare event one is issued) will be processed within 7-10 business days.
